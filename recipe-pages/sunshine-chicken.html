Sunshine Chicken

  • one tray of chicken legs, about 4-6 pounds
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced or garlic powder to taste
  • 1/2 c. soy sauce
  • 1/2 c. ketchup
  • 1/4 c. canola or corn oil
  • 1/4 c. honey
  • 1 tsp. dried basil
  • 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper
  1. Combine all ingredients except chicken in a large bowl and stir to combine.
  2. Add chicken to bowl and combine so that chicken is co with sauce. Marinate for up to a day in refrigerator.
  3. Place chicken in a single layer in a shallow baking pan and baste with sauce. Bake uncovered in a 350-degree oven for one hour.

For less fat you can remove the skin of the chicken before adding it to the marinade and it still comes out delicious. Yields 6-8 servings.
