Chicken Stew

This dish is easy and especially satisfying on a cold day!

  • 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 medium potatoes
  • 6 medium carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tbsp. sugar
  • 3 tbsp. soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp. sesame oil
  • garlic powder to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • ground red pepper to taste
  • 5-8 shiitake mushrooms (optional)
  1. If using dried mushrooms, soak them in warm water for 20 minutes or until soft. Cut off stems and slice caps into 1/4- to 1/2-inch wide strips.
  2. Put all ingredients into a large pot and stir to combine. Cover and bring to a gentle boil over medium-high heat, and then decrease heat to low. Simmer for one hour, stirring once or twice.
  3. Serve with rice and side vegetables.

If you'd like to lessen the cooking time, you can also saute everything in the pot over medium-high heat for ten minutes, and then reduce the heat to low and simmer covered for about twenty minutes. Yields 6-8 servings.
